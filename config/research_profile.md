# Research Profile

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of High Energy Physics (IHEP), Chinese Academy of Sciences, working on the Jiangmen Underground Neutrino Observatory (JUNO). My research spans theoretical particle physics, early-universe cosmology, and multi-loop perturbative calculations.

## Core Research Interests

- **Early-universe cosmology**: cosmic reheating dynamics, pre-thermalization gravitational waves, Bose-enhanced inflaton decay, stochastic gravitational-wave backgrounds
- **Primordial black holes (PBHs)**: Hawking evaporation, BBN constraints, high-energy neutrino and UHECR production from PBHs, dark sector decay channels
- **Neutrino physics**: heavy neutral leptons (HNLs) with dark decays, cosmological bounds from BBN and $\Delta N_\text{eff}$, solar antineutrinos from neutrino self-interactions, JUNO phenomenology
- **Axion and dark matter physics**: Primakoff solar axion flux calculation, axion production in astrophysical environments, axion detection strategies
- **Multi-loop perturbative QCD**: N³LO QCD corrections to top-quark decay width, semileptonic $b \to u$ decays, harmonic polylogarithms, differential equation methods
- **Electroweak precision calculations**: two-loop $\mathcal{O}(\alpha^2)$ corrections to $e^+e^- \to ZH$, Feynman diagram categorization for future Higgs factories
- **Computational tools for high-energy physics**: Feynman amplitude generation (FeAmGen.jl), Feynman integral topology construction, automated diagram and amplitude generation with UFO models

## Topics I Am Less Interested In

- Purely instrumental or detector-focused papers without new physics results
- Machine learning applications in HEP unless they introduce fundamentally novel methodology
- Lattice QCD calculations without new theoretical insight
- Astrophysical observations without clear particle-physics implications
